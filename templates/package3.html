Devi Kanya Kumari

Devi Kanya Kumari, is Shree Bhagavathy in the form of an adolescent girl child. Devi is also known as Shree Baala Bhadra or Shree Baala. She is popularly known as "Bhagavathy" (Durga or Parvati) "Devi".

Suchindrum

Suchindrum is a temple town situated in the southern most district of Kanyakumari in Tamil Nadu State, India. It is 11 km from Kanyakumari city and 7 km from Nagercoil. It is an important pilgrim center and the site of the famous Thanumalayan Temple.

Madurai

Madurai is a major city and cultural headquarters in the state of Tamil Nadu in India. It is the administrative headquarters of Madurai District and the 25th largest urban agglomeration in India.Madurai is the second largest city by area and third largest city by population in Tamil Nadu

Rameswaram

Rameswaram, is a town and a second grade municipality in the Ramanathapuram district in the South Indian state of Tamil Nadu. It is located on Pamban Island separated from mainland India by the Pamban channel and is about 50 kilometres from Mannar Island, Sri Lanka.

Tanjore Temple

The Tanjore Temple, also known as the Brihadeeswarar Temple or Peruvudaiyar Kovil, is an ancient Hindu temple located in Thanjavur (formerly known as Tanjore), Tamil Nadu, India. Built during the 11th century by the Chola dynasty, it is one of the largest and most impressive temples in South India. The temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and features stunning Dravidian architecture, with a towering vimana (temple tower) that reaches a height of 216 feet (66 meters).

Thiruvannamalai Temple

The Thiruvannamalai Temple, also known as the Arunachaleswarar Temple, is a sacred Hindu temple situated in the town of Thiruvannamalai in Tamil Nadu, India. Dedicated to Lord Shiva, it is one of the largest temples in the country and holds immense religious significance. The temple complex is situated at the base of the Annamalai Hill, which is considered a manifestation of Lord Shiva himself.

Vellore Golden temple

The Vellore Golden Temple, officially known as the Sri Lakshmi Narayani Golden Temple, is a prominent spiritual and architectural landmark located in Sripuram near Vellore, Tamil Nadu, India. It is dedicated to the goddess Sri Lakshmi Narayani, a manifestation of Goddess Lakshmi, the deity of wealth and prosperity, and Lord Narayana, a form of Lord Vishnu.

Tirupathi Temple

The Tirupati Temple, officially known as the Sri Venkateswara Temple, is a renowned Hindu temple located in the town of Tirupati in the Chittoor district of Andhra Pradesh, India. It is one of the most revered and visited pilgrimage sites in the country. The temple is dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu, and is situated atop the Tirumala Hills, surrounded by the picturesque Eastern Ghats. The temple complex is vast and encompasses several shrines, halls, and pavilions.
